What is intelligent transport system development

Intelligent transportation system is the application of analyzing, controlling and communication technologies to ground transportation to enhance safety and efficiency. Intelligent transportation system development includes applications that process and share information with the intention to ease congestion, reduce environmental impact, upgrade traffic management and increase the benefits of transportation to commercial users and the general public.

Advantage of intelligent transport system:
  • Reduced stops and delays at intersections.
  • Better speed control.
  • Improved travel time.
  • Capacity and Incident management.

OFC network design and laying

OFC or optical fibre communication holds-up digital framework of the contemporary urban network. Winsome’s fibre-optic network design team focuses on the support of developing bandwidth requirements in carrier and enterprise fibre optic networks.

Starting from fibre optic cable to reconfigurable optical add-drop multiplexer (ROADM), dense wavelength division multiplexing (DWDM) and packet-optical transport platforms, OFC network design technology continues to find its usage in the mobile backend, data center, cloud services, and other high-speed network applications.

Fibre optic network design is the specialized process leading to the successful installation and operation of a fibre optic network. One of the most important parts of HTMS solution is optical fibre communication network design. It connects all the field equipment that is part of HTMS with each other and the central control room by providing a communication link.
